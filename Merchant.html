Get listed on The Queue Platform

1. Prerequisite documents

You would need to upload legal proof of your shop, organization or complex before submitting to The Queue Platform. This process only to ensure that you are not doing illegal activities with the use of The Queue Platform.

2. Submit partner registration form

You have to fill partner registration form to get listed on The Queue Platform and display your queue. You have to visit https://partner-with-us.queue.com/become-partner and fill the details about yourself and your shop. If you unable to register at given website then you can mail us your details at mail partnerwithus@queue.com or direct message us at twitter handle @thequeue, instagram handle "thequeue".

3. The Queue calls back

If everything seems correct about your registration details, then you get call back or email to get more details about your shop. Our Queue representative should directly call back and discuss about default money and some paperwork.

4. Yeah! You are listed on The Queue

You are listed on The Queue Platform.
